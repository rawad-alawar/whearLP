About Whear

Whear was founded with a simple mission: to help people better and feel great about the clothes they are wearing. Over the past several months, we have been building a mobile platform dedicated to that goal. As the fashion industry grows its presence on social media, we believe the time has come for fashion and apparel to have their own platform.

Whear will be the social sharing platform for fashion and style. In combining the best features of the most popular social media outlets, we will:

  1. Help you dress better by getting simple feedback on your outfits from people around you
  2. Help you discover trends in your area and abroad
  3. Give you an easy way to buy clothes you like in other people’s pictures.
